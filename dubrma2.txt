Docker is a set of coupled software-as-a-service and platform-as-a-service products that use operating-system-level virtualization to develop and deliver software in packages called containers.[clarification needed][6] The software that hosts the containers is called Docker Engine.[7] It was first started in 2013 and is developed by Docker, Inc.[8] The service has both free and premium tiers.
